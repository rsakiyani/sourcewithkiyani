FAQ

Common Questions

Everything UK, GCC, and Pakistani importers typically want to know before working with us.

How is Kiyani Sourcing different from using Alibaba directly?

Alibaba shows you listings — we show you reality. We physically visit factories, verify their operations, and negotiate in person. You get verified suppliers and someone who can spot problems before they become your problem.

How do I pay Kiyani Sourcing?

We offer secure payment through our UAE bank account as well as our Chinese company bank account. Most UK, Pakistani, and GCC clients prefer paying through our UAE channel for added security and ease of international transfer.

Do you take commissions from suppliers?

Never. We are paid by you — our client — only. Taking supplier commissions creates a conflict of interest and we refuse to operate that way. Our fee structure is fully transparent and agreed upfront.

What is the minimum order size you work with?

We typically work with buyers placing orders of £5,000 to £100,000. Smaller discovery work — like supplier searches and sample coordination — can be arranged for any buyer ready to pay the flat fee.

Do you serve GCC and Pakistani importers as well as UK buyers?

Yes — this is one of our core strengths. We hold UAE residency and a Dubai bank account for GCC buyers. We have deep roots in Pakistan and understand Pakistani import culture. We serve all three markets with equal expertise.

Can you help with Canton Fair visits?

Yes — this is a key service we offer. We are based in Guangzhou where Canton Fair takes place. We can guide UK, Pakistani, and GCC traders through the fair, arrange factory visits, translate, and negotiate on your behalf.
